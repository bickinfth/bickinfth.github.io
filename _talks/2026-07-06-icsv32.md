Contributed talk (paper 801) at the [32nd International Congress on Sound and Vibration (ICSV32)](https://www.icsv32.org/), 6-10 July 2026, Istanbul.

Co-authors: N. F. Biçkin, B. E. Abali, A. F. Okyar, S. Eskandari.

The study formulates periodic elastic eigenvalue problems in Firedrake and uses PETSc eigensolvers to compute dispersion relations for two-dimensional metamaterial unit cells. It examines how geometric and material changes can shift and widen phononic band gaps for targeted vibration attenuation.
