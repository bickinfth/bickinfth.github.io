This theme focuses on **band-gap engineering**: adjusting the shape and material layout of a 2D periodic unit cell to control the frequency ranges in which elastic waves are attenuated. The Firedrake-based approach was presented as paper 801 at [ICSV32](/talks/2026-07-06-icsv32) in Istanbul.
