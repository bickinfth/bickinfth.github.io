My main research line studies how sound and elastic waves travel through **periodic metamaterial unit cells** in two dimensions. By tuning the geometry of a unit cell, band gaps — frequency ranges where waves cannot propagate — can be opened and shifted.

The modelling is built on the open-source finite element framework [Firedrake](https://www.firedrakeproject.org/) with the [PETSc](https://petsc.org/) solver stack, covering both the scalar (acoustic) and full elastic wave problems with periodic boundary conditions.
