I am a Research Assistant and integrated Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at [Yeditepe University](https://yeditepe.edu.tr/en/academic-staff/8064). My research focuses on **computational mechanics**, **finite element analysis**, and elastic-wave propagation in **two-dimensional periodic metamaterials**. Using Firedrake and PETSc, I investigate how unit-cell geometry and material distribution shape dispersion relations and phononic band gaps. My broader experience includes electro-elastodynamics, piezoelectric-acoustic interactions, and open-source finite element development with FEniCS and FEniCSx.

My work spans

* elastic-wave propagation and phononic band gaps
* computational mechanics and finite element analysis
* open-source simulation with Firedrake, PETSc, FEniCS, and FEniCSx

Please see the [Research](/portfolio/) page to find out more.
