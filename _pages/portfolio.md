My research centres on **elastic-wave propagation and band-gap formation in two-dimensional periodic metamaterials**. I use finite element models developed with the open-source [Firedrake](https://www.firedrakeproject.org/) framework and the [PETSc](https://petsc.org/) solver stack to study how unit-cell geometry and material distribution affect wave dispersion and vibration attenuation.

My earlier work includes electro-elastodynamic problems, piezoelectric-acoustic interactions, and migrating an open-source piezoelectric solver from FEniCS to FEniCSx.
